WELCOME TO CREATIVE ACADEMY

Creative Academy is a group creatives working together to start a relaxed environment to inspire the newer generation of creatives. Our major goal is to help harness skills of up-and-coming photographers. Secondly, establish an environment full of quality professional photographers who can express their creative potential and foster a healthy community. Our main mission is to teach fundamental photography, core skills and firm foundations they'll need to thrive in their field of work.

STUDIO HIRE

Our studio spaces comes with the use of in-house equipment and an array of services to make your shoot run smoothly. With availability of latest equipment, skilled and serene ambient, we can accommodate a wide variety of photography and videography needs.

COMMERCIAL SERVICES

Creative Studio Academy offers a broad range of photographic services, including planning photo shoots, editing images, hiring hair stylists, and renting out equipment. We are the perfect choice for both those who lack photographic resources and those who wish to bolster their in-house teams because we can work to your specific brief or manage projects of any size internally. We understand the influence that high-quality photography can have on a business because of our background in graphic design, which is why we are committed to offering the greatest facilities at competitive costs.

OUR SERVICES

Creative Studio Academy provides an extensive range of professional photography, graphic design, training, consulting, IT & Tech services to meet a range of corporate needs

From fashion and magazine photo shoots to large-scale film production, we are able to coordinate, carry out, and support a variety of diverse projects. With the help of our gifted staff, you may receive complete creative guidance and have material that precisely represents your needs.

Organizations and retailers might wish to think about using our eCommerce shoots as a cost-effective alternative to hiring an inhouse team in order to acquire attractive product photography. We have the professional competence to provide amazing photography that will draw in your target market thanks to our experience in fashion, beauty, and still life.
